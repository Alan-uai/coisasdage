# **App Name**: Artesã Aconchegante

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog: Browse a variety of handmade rugs and crochet kits with images, descriptions, and prices.
- Product Customization: Customize rugs and kits by selecting size, color, and material preferences before adding to cart.
- Order Tracking: Track the progress of orders from processing to shipment and delivery.
- AI Design Tool: Use an AI design tool to visualize custom rug designs based on user inputs and preferences before ordering, leveraging a tool to make creative decisions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft sage green (#A8CFAF) to evoke a sense of calm and craftsmanship.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC), very desaturated, to create a warm, inviting backdrop.
- Accent color: Pale rose (#F0B2C9), analogous to the sage green, adding a touch of subtle warmth and elegance, distinct in brightness and saturation.
- Body and headline font: 'Literata', a serif font that gives a literary and vintage feel.
- Use custom, hand-drawn-style icons for navigation and product features.
- Implement a clean, grid-based layout that highlights product imagery and customization options.
- Incorporate subtle transitions and loading animations to enhance user engagement.
